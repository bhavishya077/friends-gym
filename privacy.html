Health Data Privacy

Friends Gym uses Health Connect only when you choose to connect it.

Data we read

We request read-only access to your daily step total. We do not request write access, heart rate, medical records, location, contacts, or background health access.

How it is used

Your step total is shown in your workout dashboard and, when you are signed in, may be saved to your own protected Friends Gym activity record so it can sync across your devices.

Your control

You can deny or revoke access at any time in Android Settings > Health Connect > App permissions. Manual workout tracking remains available without Health Connect.

Sharing and deletion

We do not sell health data or use it for advertising. Your activity rows are protected by account-level access rules. You may remove a saved workout in the app or request account deletion from the gym administrator.
